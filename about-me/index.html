About Me

I am a software engineer currently working as a Teaching Assistant at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering – Skopje.

This blog is a way for me to express my thoughts, my current research and findings. I believe that teaching is the best way to learn something. I write about the problems we give to our students, occasionally I write guides or tutorials about stuff I find interesting, and think it would be helpful for other people.
I’d like your remarks on anything written here. 🙂

When I’m not teaching or writing here, I work on projects related to FinTech, Healthcare, Cryptocurrency and Blockchain, and other interesting modern technologies.
My areas of interest and research include: System Design and Reliability, Information Security, Computer Networks and Protocols, System Automation and Monitoring, Linux and more.

I am free as a consultant or project owner, and always in the mood to grab some ☕.
